Our transportation infrastructure serves 102.7 million passenger journeys annually, representing a healthy 4.8% year-over-year growth that reflects increasing public trust and utilization. The network spans 32,929 kilometers of active rail infrastructure complemented by 877,000 kilometers of road coverage, providing comprehensive connectivity across the region. System performance remains strong, with passenger satisfaction consistently maintained between 80-90%, indicating effective service delivery and operational reliability. This integrated multi-modal approach continues to support regional mobility needs while demonstrating capacity for sustained growth and quality service standards.
